About Us

The Social Initiative for Driving Development and Humanitarian Impact (SIDDHI Nepal) is a youth and women-led nonprofit organization committed to advancing inclusive, ethical, and sustainable development. Our work focuses on improving health, education, gender equality, environmental resilience, and economic opportunities for marginalized communities. We are registered under Society’s Act 2034 and with the Social Welfare Council (SWC), Nepal. Our Central Office is located at Lalitpur.

Our Mission

To drive inclusive, ethical, and sustainable development by empowering communities through education, health, research, innovation, and equitable opportunities—ensuring dignity, resilience, and well-being for all.

Our Vision

A society where communities—especially the most vulnerable—are healthy, educated, economically secure, gender-equal, and resilient to climate and social challenges.
